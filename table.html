Weather August 2017: Top 4 facts about rain

  1. London and Cambridge are the driest cities.
  2. Surprisingly, the continent that receives the lowest amount of rain and snow is Antarctica.
  3. The Oxford English Dictionary has 462 entries with “rain” in their definition from mizzle (to rain in fine droplets) to drouk (drench with heavy rain)
  4. The average raindrop takes little more than two minutes to reach the ground from a cloud.
